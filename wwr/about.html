History

Founded in 2010, Whitewater Rafting began as a small family business passionate about sharing the thrill of rafting with others.

Our mission has always been to provide safe, thrilling experiences that create lasting memories for our guests.

Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name, guiding thousands of adventurers down some of the most exciting rivers in the region. Our commitment to safety and fun remains at the core of everything we do.
